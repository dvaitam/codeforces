Description:
You are given four integers which are the pairwise sums and total sum of three unknown integers.
Each of the four integers is in random order and represents one of: x+y, x+z, y+z, and x+y+z.
Recover the original integers x, y, z.
Input Format:
One line with four integers.
Output Format:
Three integers x, y, z that satisfy the given sums.
