The ratings of all players are distinct.For each player, the most exciting match will be with the player whose rating is the closest to theirs. Formally, for the $$$i$$$-th player, the best opponent is another player $$$j$$$ such that the absolute difference in their ratings $$$|a_i-a_j|$$$ is minimized among all ways to choose player $$$j$$$. Note that some player can have more than one best opponent.For example, if there are $$$4$$$ esports players with ratings $$$[3, 7, 5, 12]$$$, then:  for player $$$1$$$, the best opponent is player $$$3$$$;  for player $$$2$$$, the best opponent is player $$$3$$$;  for player $$$3$$$, the best opponents are players $$$1$$$ and $$$2$$$;  for player $$$4$$$, the best opponent is player $$$2$$$. The organizers of the showmatch want to pair the participants so that each player is in exactly one pair, and in each pair, the players are best opponents for each other.
