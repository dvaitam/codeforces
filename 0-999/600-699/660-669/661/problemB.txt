Given a month number m (1..12), output its season.
Use the following mapping:
 Winter: December, January, February (12,1,2)
 Spring: March, April, May (3-5)
 Summer: June, July, August (6-8)
 Autumn: September, October, November (9-11)
Input begins with t, then t integers m.
Output the season name for each m on a separate line.
